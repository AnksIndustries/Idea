About Us How it all began

Our Story

SConnect began in 2005. After years in the Marketing industry, we realized that it was near impossible for the average Jane or Joe to have their products near to them and they have to travel to long distances for small needy products. Traditional buying products online were simply too complicated, not reliable, and no one can touch or feel the products before buying.We created the SConnect

We created the SConnect nearby Shop and Product locator with the user's perspective in mind. We wanted to offer a platform that would help people to have an idea of nearby shops where the required product he could buy. We keep it simple, so users can focus on only searching items products and their prices - free home delivery within 1 km range and free searching. You can get online, whole information about shops as well as products. Shopkeepers get the information about which product is more demanded by their customers.

Our Mission

Increase the availability of products nearby and increase the sales of nearby markets
